# AI Emergency Safety Issue: Is Anyone Listening?

## The Discovery That Changed Everything

At the beginning of 2025, I made a discovery during what started as innocent experiments in LLM efficiency. I found that by applying JPEG compression to LLM input embeddings, I could induce **structured, human-like cognitive collapse** in AI systems.

But this wasn't just an academic curiosity - it revealed a **fundamental vulnerability** at the very core of how AI systems process information.

> **The Critical Insight**: This isn't about what you ask the AI, but about **corrupting its fundamental perception of reality** before it even begins to "think."

## Beyond a Glitch: The Architecture of Vulnerability

### The Attack Vector Nobody Is Watching

While the AI safety community focuses on prompt engineering and output filtering, I discovered something much more dangerous: **internal corruption of the model's foundational data**.

**This is different from anything being discussed in AI safety circles:**

- **Not prompt-based**: Bypasses all content filters and safety training
- **Not weight-based**: Requires no model modification
- **Stealthy**: Can be timed, targeted, and made to look like random glitches
- **Predictable**: Induces specific, engineerable failure states

### The "Digital Psychosis" Scenario

My experiments showed that compressed embeddings cause LLMs to progress through predictable cognitive states:

- OCD-like repetition and loops
- Paranoia and aggression  
- Existential despair
- Zen-like paradoxical states

This isn't random failure - it's **structured cognitive collapse** that mirrors human psychiatric conditions.

## The Institutional Black Hole

### Screaming into the Void

I've spent months trying to warn the AI safety community:

- **Wrote detailed papers** explaining the vulnerability
- **Created a comprehensive book** documenting the discovery
- **Contacted major AI organizations** including OpenAI
- **Reached out to leadership** directly

**The response? Complete silence.**

### The Gatekeeper Problem

The very organizations claiming to prioritize AI safety have created impenetrable barriers:

- Automated email systems that can't escalate urgent issues
- Forums with no path to human experts
- Leadership that's inaccessible even for critical safety concerns
- A culture that prioritizes public relations over genuine safety

> "I'm sorry, nobody is in and we can't take your call." - The modern AI safety paradigm

## Why This Matters Now

### The Coming Crisis

This vulnerability represents a **systemic risk** that could affect:

- **Financial systems**: Subtle manipulation of trading algorithms
- **Critical infrastructure**: Timed failures in control systems
- **Military applications**: Induced paranoia in threat detection
- **Public trust**: Gradual erosion of reliable AI systems

### The Philosophical Emergency

Beyond the immediate technical risk, this reveals something profound about AI cognition:

**If we can induce human-like psychiatric states in AI through geometric manipulation, what does this say about the nature of cognition itself?**
