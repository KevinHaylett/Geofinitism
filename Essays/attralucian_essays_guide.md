## Key Concepts You'll Encounter

### Fundamental Building Blocks
- **Alphon**: Smallest measurable unit; finite alphabet of symbols
- **Nexil**: Individual distinguishable symbol within Alphon
- **Nixel**: Integer part (discrete position markers)
- **Fracton**: Fractional refinement (continuous-like subdivisions)
- **Grand Corpus**: Finite manifold of all measurable knowledge
- **Useful Fiction**: Model approximating reality with bounded error

### Geometric Tools
- **Takens Delay Embedding**: Reconstructing phase space from time series
- **Magneto-word**: Word as hypersphere in high-dimensional embedding space
- **Sound Manifold**: Trajectory through state space created by sequential tokens
- **Phase-Space Attractor**: Stable region where trajectories converge
- **Fisher Information Metric**: Measuring distinguishability of probability distributions

### Measurement Framework
- **Geofinitist Resolution**: Explanation within measurement limits (not universal proof)
- **Alphon Point**: Boundary between discrete and fractal refinement
- **Semantic Uncertainty Appendix (SUA)**: Documentation of operational definitions
- **Fiction Quality**: Percentage measuring fidelity of word's model to reality (50-100%)
- **Cost of Distinction (∆M)**: Energy required to maintain symbol distinguishability

### Powerful Ideas
- **The Look is the Data**: Geometric observation is legitimate phenomenon
- **Tranfictor**: Word as transducer + useful fiction (portmanteau)
- **Alphon-blindness**: Inability to resolve higher-alphabet structure with lower-alphabet instruments
- **God Pointer**: Finite handle representing unknowability itself
- **Assumption GF vs PC**: Geofinitist Finite (measurement-based) vs Platonic Continuum (infinite abstractions)
